Kick rocks down a sidewalk

About

Rokik is a 2D endless runner where you must move a pair of shoes individually to walk down a sidewalk as you keep a rock on the screen by kicking it, while avoiding obstacles along the way.

Rokik was my first solo game project, developed over a month as a challenge. I made it while I was learning to program, as well as using GameMaker for the first time. It was a really fun experience overall!

  • Game page: Itch.io
  • Released: 2019
  • Engine: GameMaker Studio 1.4
  • Platforms: Windows, Android
