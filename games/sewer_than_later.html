Retreat to the sewers

About

Sewer than Later is a 2D platformer where you must avoid obstacles and reach the goal in the fastest time possible.

Developed over 3 days for the "STOP WAITING FOR GODOT" challenge, the core auto-running mechanic found in the game takes cues from games like RobTop's "Geometry Dash", and the level design was inspired by EXOK's "Celeste", as well as the game's focus on improving your times on beaten levels.
Upon initial release, the game became very popular amongst other participants of the jam, and within its first week it had over 500 players. This motivated the team and I to briefly continue working on it after the jam.
